Math Club

Westtown Math Club is a student-run club at Westtown School focusing on the study of mathematics. We are a group of people who enjoy the problem-solving aspect of mathematics. Our club is open to all students at Westtown and contains people at various levels of mathematical knowledge. In the fall, we participate in Math Madness, which is a competiton where students solve AMC-style problems and compete with local schools to see who can solve the most. We do this during most meetings.
